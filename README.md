# NoAddRepCost

This is a simple mod which disables the additional repair costs system in minecraft with anvils. 
No more "Too expensive" in your anvil gui. You can now combine your enchanted books with your hundreds of enchantments without needing 100 levels or receiving the message "Too Expensive".
Perfect for overpowered tools.

Curseforge: https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/no-additional-repair-cost
